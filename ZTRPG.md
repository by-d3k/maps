# Zelshiky Tribe RPG

---

# **Story Dialogue**

---

## **Scene 1 — Arrival at Slenzar**

**[Slenzar castle — candle-lit interior]**

**PRIEST CIDEL:** It's nice to see new faces around Slenzar. Are you all well?  

**PARTY LEADER:** After a good night's rest, we should be fine.  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** You seem like a healthy lot. If you seek work, my militia could always use more good men…  

**PARTY LEADER:** No, we will only be staying for the night...  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** Well then! Stay here at Slenzar as long as you must.  
I have important matters to deal with. I will speak with you later...

---

## **Scene 2 — The Attack**

**[Town square — alarms ringing outside]**

**PRIEST CIDEL:** Travelers! How glad I am to see you! I assume you heard the madness outside?  

**PARTY LEADER:** What's going on?  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** The Zelshiky Tribe has assembled an army and are outside Slenzar about to attack.  
They have come to destroy our town, and I fear our defenses may fail...  

**PARTY LEADER:** We can help you. We may seem like simple travelers, but we are very capable of holding our own in battle.  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** Well... I never doubted it for a second. Help my forces on the frontlines.  
Fend them off as long as possible.  

**RANGER:** Sir, we must go now.  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** Well, I'm off. Fight hard, travelers. You face an evil with countless ruthless followers. Good luck.

---

## **Scene 3 — Aftermath & Revelation**

**[Inside a cave — survivors gathered, wounded murmuring]**

**PRIEST CIDEL:** That bastard! How dare he unleash his heathens on my town!  
Look what he has done — he has killed everything in his path… what a bloody mess!  

**SISTER MANELLA:** His sins are watched closely and will be judged by God.  
Such treacherous acts will send him to hell swiftly.  
His attempt on your life was a close one. I suggest we comply with God's will by retaliating.  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** I agree, but we don't even know where he and his tribe have set camp.  
My men have searched high and low… we have made slow progress.  
He hides himself well — the coward!  

**PARTY LEADER:** Excuse us… who is this you speak of?  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** His name is Zelk, the leader of the forsaken Zelshiky Tribe… decrepit people.  
And yet, still, they find time to terrorize my own…  

**PARTY LEADER:** Must we retreat to this cave? Can we not fight back?  

**SISTER MANELLA:** They have come in search of something.  
When they see it is not here, they will leave and go elsewhere.  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** …  

**SISTER MANELLA:** Ah— yes. The people must be growing weary and need food and water.  
I will tend to their suffering.  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** I thank you all for being of assistance.  
Please forgive me… I must take this time to think.

---

## **Scene 4 — Rebuilding**

**[Ruins of Slenzar — smoke rising, villagers searching debris]**

**PRIEST CIDEL:** Those animals destroyed everything… every last thing…  

**PARTY LEADER:** We're sorry. We fought them as long as we could, but they were too strong…  
I suppose the situation calls for more than an apology...  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** There is little hope… but I believe we can fix this.  

**PARTY LEADER:** Right. We’ll help the men salvage whatever they can from the wreckage  
and hunt for food so they may feed their families.  

**PRIEST CIDEL:** Little things like that, although gracious, will not solve this problem.  
We must look East, to the town of Gixon.  
There you will find a wise King. Tell him of our troubles.  
He is a generous man — he will listen.  
He can resupply us.  
Then, we rebuild… and begin anew.

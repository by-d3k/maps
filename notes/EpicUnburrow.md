# Epic Unburrow

-----

Download: [EpicUnburrow](../files/EpicUnburrow.scx)

-----

## Objectives 

A large number of zerg units will unburrow in the center of the map. Use your Dark Archon to mind control as many as you can, including some special units. 

Use your mind controlled units to kill as many other units as possible.

Reach a certain kill count to win.

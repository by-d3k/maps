## Games

There are 19 mini-games you can play:

#,Mini-Game Name,Objective
1,Maze,Find the exit.
2,Glitchy Wall Jump,Jump over crystals without touching purple ones.
3,Pattern Puzzle,Kill civilians in the correct order.
4,Fire Path,Reach the end without touching fire.
5,Lure,Lure 10 Archons into explosions.
6,Perfect Smiley,Create a pylon smiley face using exactly 11 pylons.
7,Blood Bath,Kill burrowed units by attacking your own Medic.
8,Stimulation,Move back and forth quickly to collect all eggs.
9,Yellow Rines,Count yellow-flashing Marines and siege your tank to guess.
10,Mine Field,Place at least 100 mines in the area without hitting them.
11,Lurker Splash,Kill 5 Dark Templars using Lurker splash damage.
12,BullzEye,Siege your tank 5 times during red explosions.
13,Dodge The Ling,"Collect 1,000 minerals while dodging Zerglings."
14,5 Bounds,Navigate 5 levels of explosion obstacles.
15,Collectin Scouts,Bump into Scouts to reach 10 points; avoid Wraiths.
16,Missile Launching,Use Dark Swarm to fire missiles and kill all enemy units.
17,Warm & Cold,"Find a hidden Infested Terran using ""Warm/Cold"" text hints."
18,Mixture Madness,Kill specific Lurkers to reach a balanced resource count.
19,The Cave of Doom,Reach the end of the cave area while surviving.
20,Invisible Path,Navigate a hidden walkway to the end.
21,Obstacle Course,Reach the end of the course while dodging traps.
22,Nuclear Launch Detected,Survive an area being targeted by nuclear strikes.
23,Consolidation,Place exactly 24 units on a single point.
24,Mystic Templar,Reach the Templar location to clear the area.
25,Ghost Micro,"Navigate a Ghost to the end point (""End Ghost Micro"")."

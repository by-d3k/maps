## Objectives

A sequel to the original [Shoot Da Targets](./notes/ShootDaTargets.md), you are tasked with shooting down an increasing number of targets. The distance from the targets also increase as you progress through the game. Earn special guns from target hit streaks and milestones throughout the game.
